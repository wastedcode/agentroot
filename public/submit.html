Submit Your Domain

AgentRoot checks your domain for _agent and _skill TXT records and indexes whatever it finds. No signup required.

🤖

Registering an Agent?

Add a _agent.yourdomain.com TXT record. Agents are autonomous entities with endpoints and capabilities.

🔧

Publishing a Skill?

Add a _skill.yourdomain.com TXT record. Skills are callable tools — MCP, OpenAPI, REST, GraphQL, gRPC.
